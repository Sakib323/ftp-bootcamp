Future Talent Program

The Future Talent Programme is a free, annual, three-month bootcamp organized by Qbitlab to teach students about science and technology and raise their awareness of technological advancements. Through hands-on projects using components like Arduino and Raspberry Pi, we provide a platform to encourage STEM education in Bangladesh.
